Pig Fat

By moving the fat to the outside of the leg, our bodies are cushioned and restricted within our crates. The efficient separation between fat and muscle allows for quicker handling of our corpses on the slaughter line, minimizing cost and labor. Although fat is as heavy as muscle, my legs feel extremely heavy because of the sagging nature of the fat, which makes meet pulled down towards the ground and extremely bulky, making standing a burden. Because we are made to only stand up within our crates, life, therefore, is burdensome.
